Increasing Difficulty: As the snake grows longer, the game becomes progressively challenging. Players must navigate the snake through increasingly complex paths while avoiding collisions. Collision Detection: The game incorporates collision detection mechanisms to determine if the snake collides with itself or the game boundaries. Collisions lead to the game's conclusion. Score Tracking: "Snake Mania" keeps track of the player's score based on the number of food items consumed. Players strive to achieve higher scores with each playthrough. Visual Elements: Pygame's graphical capabilities allow for the creation of visually appealing graphics, including the snake, food items, and game area. Audio Effects: "Snake Mania" can feature sound effects through Pygame, enhancing the gaming experience with auditory feedback. Overall, "Snake Mania" presents a modern take on the classic snake game, leveraging Python's programming capabilities and Pygame's graphical and interactive features.
